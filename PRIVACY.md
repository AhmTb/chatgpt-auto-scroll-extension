# Privacy

ChatGPT Auto Scroll is designed to stay local.

- It does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your ChatGPT messages.
- It does not call external servers.
- It does not use analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, or advertising.
- It stores only one local browser setting: whether auto-scroll is enabled.
- It requests only the `storage` extension permission.
- Its content script is limited to `chatgpt.com` and `chat.openai.com`.

The extension reads the ChatGPT page structure only to detect the active chat surface, send controls, and scroll position. That information is not sent anywhere.
